Project Team

TRUSTED is driven by a collaborative and multidisciplinary team comprising experts from Newcastle University and University of Liverpool, both renowned for their contributions to cutting-edge research in cybersecurity and formal methods. Partnering with industry, the Google's security team and OCamlPro contribute invaluable real-world insights and practical experience to the project.
